I'm a PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Andrej Risteski. Prior to CMU, I was an undergraduate at Georgia Tech, where I worked on computation and inference with Florian Schäfer.

My research interests are in problems spanning machine learning, statistics, and applied mathematics. I'm particularly interested in efficient numerical algorithms for generative modeling, sampling, and statistical inference. See these high-level summaries for more details about my work.
